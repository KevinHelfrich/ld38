Dungeon World
================================
Use the arrow keys to move around, bumping into enemies and walls deals damage to them, bumping into doors opens them.
To stand still and advance the world by a turn press space.
To pick up a weapon just stand on it, it'll be swapped with whatever you are holding.
Enemies move in predictable patterns, you need to time your attacks to avoid taking damage.
If you are adjacent to an enemy on a turn it would normally move, it will instead deal damage to you.
